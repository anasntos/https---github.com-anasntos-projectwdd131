Safe Song - Site Plan

Site Name

Safe Song
Logo Placeholder Here

This name was chosen because it reflects the core mission of the site: to provide music that is safe and uplifting, keeping listeners close to the Holy Spirit without distractions. It is memorable, simple, and meaningful to the target audience.

Optional domain availability: safesong.org

Site Purpose

The Safe Song website serves as a platform to share and discover music that inspires spiritual connection and peace. It offers curated playlists, new song submissions, and resources for those seeking uplifting music that aligns with their faith and values.

Scenarios

Here are examples of questions visitors might have, guiding the website content:

  • Where can I find songs that help me feel closer to the Holy Spirit during my daily routine?
  • How can I submit a new song that fits the Safe Song philosophy?

Color Schema

The selected color scheme for Safe Song is:

  • #2c3e50 (Dark Blue) — used for headings, main titles, and important accents.
  • #e67e22 (Warm Orange) — used for buttons, links, highlights, and call-to-actions.

This combination creates a calm yet inviting atmosphere, balancing professionalism with warmth.

Typography

Fonts chosen for the website:

  • #1e3a5f (Dark Blue) — used for headers and main titles.
  • #3c82d9 (Light Blue) — used for links and hover highlights.
  • #7a5cfa (Soft Purple) — used for accents and buttons.
  • #e0d7fa (Lilac Surface) — used for background blocks.
  • #ffffff (White) — general background color.

This color palette was selected to feel calm, spiritual, and uplifting, while maintaining high contrast and accessibility (AA/AAA compliant).
